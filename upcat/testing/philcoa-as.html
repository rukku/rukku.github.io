From Philcoa

to Palma Hall

by BEST-Transit

Philcoa

Take the UP-Philcoa Jeep. The terminal is located at the back of McDonald’s. At times, drivers also wait for passengers out front.

When in a hurry, you can also opt to ride UP-SM North and UP-Pantranco jeeps unloading passengers in the area.



The jeep enters UP Diliman through the University Avenue. The first landmark you'll see is the checkpoint. This is quite noticeable as all vehicles slowdown at the guard house near the checkpoint.

The jeep then heads towards the Oblation Plaza until it reaches the Academic Oval, a one-way busy thoroughfare lined by lots of huge Acacia trees.

From the Oblation it will pass by the Vargas Museum. Prepare to get off as the jeep is now very close to Palma Hall.

Palma Hall

There are waiting sheds on both sides of Palma Hall (more popularly known as AS).

You may alight at the Faculty Center (FC) waiting shed near the Palma Hall east wing.

Alternatively, you may also alight at the next stop located near the Palma Hall west wing.
