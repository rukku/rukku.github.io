About Us

All of us were friends in UP Diliman. A couple of years back we got to design the BAYAN-e-HAN disaster risk management web application that won in the 2010 UP BEST Tech Tournament.  We wanted to get together for something useful. Reigna mentioned that there’s a lot of buzz with regard to Transportation recently and how technology can be leveraged to help. That plus our penchant for late night work and brainstorming over cafe/dinner meet ups led to the UPCAT MAPS site.
