Katipunan Jeepney Terminal

From Marikina, alight at Aurora Blvd corner Katipunan and walk towards the jeepney terminal under the Katipunan Flyover.

If you’re coming from the LRT-2 Katipunan Station, take the North Exit on the same side as St. Bridget, opposite PSBA. Walk towards the jeepney terminal under the Katipunan Flyover.

There are two queues calling UP at the terminal. Make sure you ride the jeep which goes inside the campus (the jeep line closer to Ministop). The other jeep line (farther from Ministop) goes to Tandang Sora passing through MWSS and will not go inside UP. To be sure, ask the barker before boarding.

The UP-Katipunan jeep enters UP Diliman through the gate opposite MWSS (Manila Water).Upon entry to UP, you'll see the GT Toyota Building (white) on the left.





The Jeep will have traversed most of its route inside UP before it reaches the stop nearest the Institute of Biology (Pav 4) so prepare for a long ride ahead!It will pass by establishments such as the UP Health Service, and University Shopping Center, as well as several dormitories.



The jeep will pass by several establishments, colleges, and dormitories, as well as notable UP landmarks such as the UP Carillon.

The jeep will also circuit the Academic Oval and pass by the Oblation plaza.

Palma Hall waiting shed



Get off at the Palma Hall stop (known as AS shed). Take the street beside Palma Hall. You will walk past CASAA (to your left).

Institute of Biology (Pavilion 4)

The Institute of Biology (Pav 4) is located at the back of Palma Hall (within the same block). Entrance to the Institute of Biology (Pav 4) is through Llamas Hall.

If you’re running late and there’s too much traffic:

The Palma Hall jeepney stop is one of the last stops of the UP-Katipunan jeep inside UP before the jeep goes back to Katipunan again.

If you do not have time to spare (10-15 minutes before call time), alight at the foot bridge along Katipunan Avenue connecting the old UP Integrated School compound to the main campus. There is a lesser known pedestrian entrance here marked by a guard house. You are essentially at the side of UP College of Home Economics.

Cross the foot bridge, go through the pedestrian entrance, and make a run for it. You only need to run straight toward Institute of Biology (Pav 4) from here. Entrance to Institute of Biology (Pav 4) is through Llamas Hall. Aja!
