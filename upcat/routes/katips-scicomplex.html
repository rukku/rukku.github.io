LRT-2 Katipunan or Marikina

Go to the Katipunan Jeepney Terminal


If you are from Marikina:
Riding a jeep from Marikina, alight at Aurora Blvd corner Katipunan Road. Then walk towards the UP-Katipunan Jeepney Terminal (this is under the Katipunan flyover).

If you are from LRT 2- Katipunan station:
From the LRT-2 Station at Katipunan, make sure you go out through the North Exit so that you'll end up at the opposite side of PSBA and the same block as St. Bridget. Walk towards the Katipunan Flyover; underneath it is the UP-Katipunan Jeepney Terminal.

At the UP-Katipunan Jeep Terminal, you'll see 2 queues, both calling UP. Make sure you ride the jeep which goes inside the campus (the jeep line closer to Ministop). To be sure, confirm with the jeep dispatcher that the jeep goes inside UP. (The other jeep queue that is farther from ministop goes to Tandang Sora passing-by MWSS and will not go inside UP.) Then give your fare to the dispatcher before going inside the jeep.
Fare: 8:00


Romulo Hall


The UP-Katipunan jeep enters UP Diliman through the gate opposite MWSS (Manila Water).

Vinzon's Waiting Shed

Step off from the jeep at the line of waiting sheds in front of Vinzon's Hall. From here you will be navigating on foot for about 500 meters. This is the fastest and most straightforward route to the complex.
Facing Vinzon's Hall, head to your right and walk down A. Ma. Regidor Street. This road will take you past the College of Home Economics and the PAGASA Observatory, to finally intersect with the National Science Complex.

Institute of Chemistry

After you walk past the Observatory, the Institute of Chemistry building should be to your left, before A. Ma. Regidor St. slopes down and intersects with the National Science Complex oval. The building has an unmistakable facade of bonded atoms.

National Institute of Physics

Facing the National Science Complex Amphitheater, the National Institute of Physics should be visible behind it on its right-hand side. The building has a rough tan finishing and a characteristic cylindrical annex. To get there continue walking left of the amphitheater. The left fork will lead you to the building's side entry.

Institute of Mathematics

Facing the National Science Complex Amphitheater, the Institute of Mathematics is located behind it on its left-hand side. However, the building may not be completely visible as it stands on lower terrain.
Walk down the road between the Amphitheater and the Computational Science Research Center. At the junction, the Institute of Mathematics should now be visible with its green roof and rough gray finishing. A path for pedestrians leads to the Institute's parking lot and entrance.
