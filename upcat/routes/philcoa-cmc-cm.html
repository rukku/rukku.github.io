Philcoa

Alight at Philcoa foot bridge near Petron and cross to the side of McDonald's.

Take the UP-Philcoa Jeep. The terminal is located at the back of McDonald’s. At times, drivers also wait for passengers out front.

When in a hurry, you can also opt to ride UP-SM North and UP-Pantranco jeeps unloading passengers in the area.

The Oblation Plaza

Alight from the jeep at the Oblation plaza.



The College of Mass Comm and College of Music are the last stops in the outbound route of the UP-Philcoa jeep. Walking on foot from the Oblation will take you there faster.
