SM North EDSA

Take the UP-SM North Jeep. The fair is 11 pesos.
Jeep enters UP Diliman through the University Ave (towards the Oblation), leading to the Academic Oval (oval-shaped one-way busy thoroughfare lined by lots of huge trees).

College of Mass Communication
College of Music


Alight from the jeep at the Oblation plaza. The College of Mass Communication and the College of Music are located around 215 meters to your left when facing the statue of the Oblation. The two colleges are beside each other..
